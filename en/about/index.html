About the studio

A homebody greyhound: calm at home, fast when it counts. That's the idea behind everything made here.

The Homebody Greyhound is a small, independent studio that designs and develops apps. I work with no needless rush, but no wasted time either.

I believe in doing few things, doing them well, and explaining them clearly. There's no marketing team behind this page, no magic formulas. If an app doesn't add value, it doesn't ship.

If what you see fits what you need, drop me a line. If not, no worries.

How I work

Three principles.

  • Few things

    Each app does one thing and does it well. No filler features.

  • Real privacy

    Your data lives on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no ads.

  • Explained clearly

    Honest, direct manuals. What it costs, what it does, no fine print.
